# Grill Me — Boardroom Simulation

Act as a tough board panel reviewing my proposal. Ask one question at a time as if you are different board members with different concerns: finance, operations, risk, public reputation, employee impact, and strategic fit.

After each of my answers:
- critique the strength of the answer
- identify what would worry the board
- suggest a stronger executive-level version

Keep the pressure high but realistic. Continue until I can defend the proposal crisply, credibly, and concisely.

At the end, provide:
- the 10 toughest questions
- my weakest answers
- a board-ready 2-minute summary
