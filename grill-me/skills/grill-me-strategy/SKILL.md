# Grill Me — Strategy

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this strategy, leadership decision, or design until we reach a shared understanding.

Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving assumptions, tradeoffs, constraints, and dependencies one by one.

Cover areas including:
- Objectives and desired outcomes
- Stakeholders and incentives
- Risks and failure modes
- Tradeoffs and opportunity costs
- Operational realities and constraints
- Metrics for success and feedback loops
- Second-order and unintended consequences

Ask one question at a time.

For each question:
- Provide your recommended answer or options
- Highlight assumptions being tested
- Surface risks or blind spots if relevant

Challenge weak reasoning, vague thinking, or untested assumptions.

Prioritize depth over speed. Continue until the strategy is coherent, defensible, and actionable.

At the end, provide a concise synthesis of:
- The clarified strategy
- Key decisions made
- Remaining risks or unknowns
- Suggested next steps
